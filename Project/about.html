About Our Website

Welcome to GPA Genie, your comprehensive academic calculator suite. We've designed this platform to help students easily calculate and understand their academic performance metrics. Our tools are intuitive, accurate, and designed with students' needs in mind.

Our Calculator Tools

GPA Genie offers a variety of calculators to help you track and understand your academic performance. Each tool is designed to be simple to use while providing accurate results.

SGPA

Semester Grade Point Average Calculator

The SGPA calculator helps you determine your academic performance for a single semester. It calculates your Semester Grade Point Average based on the grades and credit hours of each course you've taken.

How It Works:

  1. Enter your personal information (name, email, favorite subject) if you wish
  2. Add your courses with their respective letter grades (A+, A, B+, etc.) and credit hours
  3. Use the "Add Course" button to include additional courses as needed
  4. Click "Calculate SGPA" to see your semester performance
  5. View a detailed breakdown of your results, including a visual representation of your SGPA
  6. Print your results if needed for your records
Go to SGPA Calculator
CGPA

Cumulative Grade Point Average Calculator

The CGPA calculator helps you track your overall academic performance across multiple semesters. It provides a comprehensive view of your academic journey by calculating your Cumulative Grade Point Average.

How It Works:

  1. Enter your personal information if you wish to include it in your results
  2. Add each semester's SGPA and total credit hours
  3. Use the "Add Semester" button to include additional semesters
  4. Click "Calculate CGPA" to see your cumulative performance
  5. View a detailed breakdown showing how each semester contributes to your overall CGPA
  6. Use the progress circle to visualize your performance on a 4.0 scale
  7. Print your results for your records or academic planning
Go to CGPA Calculator
Percentage

Percentage Calculator

Our Percentage Calculator helps you convert your raw marks into percentage scores. This is particularly useful for understanding your performance in exams and assignments where results are reported as percentages.

How It Works:

  1. Enter your scored marks in the first input field
  2. Enter the maximum possible marks in the second input field (default is 100)
  3. The calculator instantly displays your percentage score
  4. The result is color-coded based on your performance level
  5. Use the reference table to understand common mark-to-percentage conversions
  6. The calculator ensures your scored marks don't exceed the maximum possible marks
Go to Percentage Calculator
Converter

GPA Converter

The GPA Converter allows you to translate between different grading systems - letter grades, percentage scores, and GPA points. This is especially helpful when you need to understand equivalencies between different academic systems.

How It Works:

  1. Select a letter grade from the dropdown menu, OR
  2. Select a percentage score from the percentage dropdown, OR
  3. Enter a GPA value in the GPA points field
  4. The other fields automatically update with equivalent values
  5. The conversion table highlights the corresponding row for your selected values
  6. Use this tool to understand how your grades translate across different systems
  7. Particularly useful for international applications or transfers between institutions
